Cults

So, what is a cult anyway?

Ben Edwards at Detroit Baptist claims that a cult has the following characteristics:

  1. Unorthodox teachings
  2. Strong internal conformity to teachings of some authority
  3. A high level of commitment from the members
  4. Strong focus on personal needs and concerns of followers

A Few Examples...

  • The Manson Family (murders)
  • The Church of Scientology
  • Heaven's Gate (drank the Kool-Aid)
  • Cults of personality (e.g. North Korea and Kim Jong Un)
